How to Convert Videos to Text

1 Find Your Video

Locate the video you want to convert on YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter. Copy the video URL from your browser's address bar or share options.

2 Paste the URL

Paste the video URL into the input field above. The tool will automatically detect the platform, but you can manually select it if needed.

3 Convert to Text

Click the "Convert to Text" button. Our tool will process the video and extract the audio content as text using advanced speech recognition technology.

For best accuracy: The tool uses your AssemblyAI API key for professional-grade transcription quality.

4 Copy or Download

Once processed, you can copy the text to your clipboard or download it as a text file for offline use, research, or content creation.
